About Us

Learn about Sepik Fresh and our mission

Who We Are

Sepik Fresh is a poultry and egg distribution company based in Wewak, East Sepik Province, Papua New Guinea. We connect local farmers with customers across the region through a reliable, technology-driven delivery system.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, locally sourced poultry and eggs with real-time order tracking, ensuring every customer receives their delivery on time and in perfect condition.

Our Values

  • Freshness and quality
  • Transparency and trust
  • Supporting local farmers
  • Reliable service
